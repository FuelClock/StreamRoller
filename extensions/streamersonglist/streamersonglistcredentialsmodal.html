StreamerSongList Credential Settings
To get your StreamerSongList client_id is a bit tricky as there isn't currently an easy way to find it from what I can find.
The only way I have found is to visit and login to the StreamerSongList webpage and then inspect the code to find it.
  1. Go to http://streamersonglist.com and log in with your details.
  2. Right click on the webpage and select the 'inspect' option at the bottom of the list
  3. In the window the pops up click on the menu item "Application"
  4. In the left side click on 'Storage' -> 'Local Storage' 'https://www.streamersonglist.com/'
  5. in the right hand side it should show the credentials needed below.
  6. Copy and paste them below ('StreamerSonglist_authToken' is the long one that goes in the authtoken field)
If anyone knows a better way to get these details please let me know so I can update these instructions
